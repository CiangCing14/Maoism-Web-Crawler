<a href='https://maoistroad.blogspot.com/2023/02/turkey-urgent-call-for-help-we-stand.html'>Turkey -  URGENT CALL FOR HELP!  WE STAND WITH OUR PEOPLE IN THE EARTHQUAKE ZONE</a>

<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4839982639776108502' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<p>&nbsp;</p><p class="western" lang="de-DE" style="margin-left: 0cm; margin-top: 0.21cm; text-indent: 0cm;">
<span style="font-size: x-small;"><span lang="en-GB">The 7.7
magnitude earthquake that struck in Pazarcık district of Maraş at
around 04:20 on Monday morning, February 6, was felt in Maraş, as
well as Malatya, Mardin, Bingöl, Elazığ, Siirt, Sivas, Van, Muş,
Bitlis, Hakkari, Adana, Osmaniye, Batman, Urfa, Kilis, Diyarbakır,
Antep, Hatay, northern Syria and Rojava provinces. The earthquake
caused extensive damage and loss</span></span><span style="font-size: x-small;"><span style="letter-spacing: 2.0pt;"><span lang="en-GB">
</span></span></span><span style="font-size: x-small;"><span lang="en-GB">of
life. According to the latest official statements, thousands of
people lost their lives in the Turkish Kurdistan region alone, while
around 6,000 people were injured. In addition to the destruction of
many houses, buildings and hospitals, airports and roads have also
been severely damaged, and with the addition of winter conditions,
rescue efforts are becoming even more difficult. In addition, as the
aftershocks continue, new earthquakes are occurring and there are
still many people under the rubble. Rescue and relief teams have not
yet reached many areas, and the people continue rescue efforts with
their own strength. Therefore, it is a reality that the number of
people killed and injured is much higher than the official figures.</span></span></p>

the founder of the<br>

the period of Liberation War of East Bengal<br>
against the national
oppressor state of Pakistan. The then main bourgeois party Awami
League<br>
fled to india, leaving people helpless under the genocide
of Pakistani genocide. Before that, at<br>
the eve of genocide,
their main leader Sheikh Mujibur Rahman surrendered to the
Pakistanis.<br>

India, for their own self-interest of power, while fleeing there.<br>

danger. Contrary to fake Indian-Awami<br>
liberation war, they waged
an actual/revolutionary war of liberation in various parts of
the<br>
country. Siraj Sikder, the founder of our party, in a
situation of absence of power, organizing a<br>
revolutionary army
at Peyarabagan area in Barishal, created a short-lived liberated
area. There<br>
he founded the &quot;Proletarian Party&quot; on June
3. Earlier during Pakistani rule, he founded &quot;East<br>
Bengal
Workers&#39; Movement&quot; as a preparatory organisation for founding a
party of its kind. He<br>
transformed it into the Shorbohara Party,
a proletarian party, in the middle of the sound of<br>
shelling of
cannon in 1971.<br>

founding of the party today, we must<span></span></span></p>

Ministério Público, pelo financiamento de pistoleiros na região para a

<div class='post hentry uncustomized-post-template' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='https://maoistroad.blogspot.com/2023/02/announcement-on-occasion-of-death.html'> Announcement on the occasion of the death comrade Farhad - CC/C(M)PAfghanistan</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4021015706664238031' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<h1 align="right" class="western" dir="rtl">
<span class="sd-abs-pos" style="left: -1.27cm; position: absolute; top: -1.27cm; width: 93px;"><img border="0" height="88" name="Picture 3" src="file:///C:/Users/user/AppData/Local/Temp/lu172642uy8du.tmp/lu172642uy8ea_tmp_615708885fed1fb1.jpg" width="93" />&nbsp;</span><span class="sd-abs-pos" style="left: 16.47cm; position: absolute; top: -1.22cm; width: 125px;">
</span><br />

</h1>
<p align="center" style="background: #ffc000; letter-spacing: -0.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0cm; margin-left: -1.27cm; margin-right: -1.22cm; text-indent: 1.27cm;">
<span style="font-family: Calibri Light, serif;"><span style="font-size: x-large;"><span style="color: white;"><b>Announcement
on the occasion of the death of Comrade</b></span><b> </b><span style="color: red;"><u><b>Farhad</b></u></span></span></span></p>
<p align="center" dir="rtl" style="border-bottom: 1px solid #000000; border-left: none; border-right: none; border-top: none; line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.28cm; padding-bottom: 0.04cm; padding-left: 0cm; padding-right: 0cm; padding-top: 0cm;">
<span style="font-family: Microsoft PhagsPa, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">(1957
&#8211; 2023)</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">The
Central Committee of the C(M)PA with deep sorrow informs all cadres,
members, supporters of the C(M)PA, other Afghan Maoists, and Maoist
parties and organizations in other countries of the world of the news
of the death of Comrade &#8220;Farhad&#8221;, a member of the second regional
committee of the C(M)PA, that passed away due to a heart attack on
Friday, January 27, 2023, at the age of 65.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Comrade
&#8220;Farhad&#8221; was born in 1957 And by tolerating all the sufferings,
hardness and difficulties completed the university with a bachelor's
degree in 1980. During his studies, he joined the Shola-e Javid's
defenders&#8217; group.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Comrade
&#8220;Farhad&#8221; started his struggles against the effete regime of the
royal family in his youth, and after the disgraceful Saur coup of
1357 SH (April Coup, April 27&#8211;28, 1978) and following it on
December 1979 with the naked Invasion of the Russian
social-imperialist forces on Afghanistan and their occupation of the
country, He rose up to serious opposition against 7<sup>th</sup> Saur
coup plotters and continued their political, national-democratic and
revolutionary activities.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Comrade
Farhad was chased in 1981 and settled in his hometown which was under
the control of Mujahideen parties and left the country in 1982 and
fled to Iran. during his struggles was in close contact with one of
his comrades at &#8220;The Revolutionary Union of Afghanistan Workers&#8221;
and regularly cooperated with him.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">After
the occupation of Afghanistan on October 7, 2001, by the imperialist
occupiers led by the occupying US imperialism, he rose up against the
unbridled imperialist occupiers and their puppet regime and began its
regular struggles.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">When
the process of unification of the communist movement
(Marxist-Leninist-Maoist) started in 2002, supported it, and after
the approval of the program and constitution of the C(M)PA on May 1,
2004, confirmed it and accepted the membership of the party.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">In
2008, he became a member of Regional Committee Number 15, and in
2009, he took responsibility for the Cultural Committee of Regional
Committee Number 15 and advanced this position well since 2011. when
the ideological-political struggle between the C(M)PA and the
Revolutionary Communist Party of America (RCP) intensified in 2011,
he firmly supported the position of the C(M)PA, and called the
&#8216;&#8216;Avakian's new syntheses&#8217;&#8217; humanistic and revisionist and
rejected it.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">When
the seminar of the party against the post-Marxist-Leninist-Maoist
line proposed in the manifesto and constitution of the Revolutionary
Communist Party of America was held in the region, Comrade Farhad
along with other comrades of the regional committee took
responsibility for the security of the seminar and until the end of
the seminar, this duty carried out well.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">In
2014, he could not participate in the second congress of the party
due to his illness, but with his message, he fully supported the
process of the second congress of the C(M)PA.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">After
the successful processing of the second All-party congress, comrade
Farhad became a member of the regional committee number (02). He
carried this responsibility well since 2019. when the issue of the
Qatar peace process was raised in 2019, Comrade Farhad took a
decisive and principled stance against the Taliban at the level of
regional number two.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">After
Comrade Zia's death, he gave the youth fighting morale and always
reminded them that the young generation should fill the empty places
of the old comrades and advised the comrades that new blood should
flow in the veins of the party.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">He
participated in the first anniversary of Comrade Zia's death, which
was organized at the regional level, and wrote a poem about Comrade
Zia under the title &#8220;You're gone and the grass went away from the
spring field.&#8221; which is included in the Persian part of party's
website and publications.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Comrade
Farhad was a capable poet, critic, and pundit and a good writer for
the party's publications. He discussed the issues of the day from an
ideological-political point of view in every meeting.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Unfortunately,
death did not allow this valuable comrade, and was taken from us at
the age of 65 due to a heart attack on Friday, January 27, 2023.</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><b>Comrades</b>:</span></span></p>
<p align="justify" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Let's
turn the sadness caused by the death of Comrade "Farhad"
into a struggling force and keep his memory as a Maoist revolutionary
alive and lasting.</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><b>May
his memory be blessed and may his path be bright and red!</b></span></span></p>
<p align="center" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><b>Central
Committee</b></span></span></p>
<p align="center" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><b>Communist
(Maoist) Party of Afghanistan</b></span></span></p>
<p align="center" lang="en-GB" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.49cm; margin-top: 0.49cm;">
<span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><b>January
27, 2023</b></span></span></p><br /><p align="justify" style="line-height: 100%; margin-bottom: 0.28cm;">
<br />

</p>

<a href='https://maoistroad.blogspot.com/2023/02/pcimaoist-contra-la-corporatizacion.html'>PCI(Maoist) - CONTRA LA CORPORATIZACIÓN, MILITARIZACIÓN E HINDUIZACIÓN  - l'appel y el supporte de ICSPWI</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-3225130398375630957' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-full"><b>I<a href="https://www.revolucionobrera.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/India-copia.jpg">CSPWI ' es el momento más favorable para desarrollar y consolidar el movimiento de solidaridad revolucionario indio en apoyo de la guerra popular en todo el mundo'</a></b></figure><figure class="aligncenter size-full"><b>10/100/1000 acciones de supporte!</b></figure><figure class="aligncenter size-full"><b>23/24/25 febrero jornadas de luchas contra la guerra imperialista<span class="Y2IQFc" lang="es"> - traer en estos días y en todas las</span><span class="Y2IQFc" lang="es"> acciones contra la guerra la importancia del apoyo a la guerra popular en la India y en el mundo! '</span></b></figure><figure class="aligncenter size-full"></figure><figure class="aligncenter size-full"><img alt="India: Condenar los ataques fascistas sin precedentes con aviones no tripulados y helicópteros de combate del gobierno central y del estado de Chhattisgad 1" class="wp-image-16201" height="221" src="https://www.revolucionobrera.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/India-copia.jpg" width="400" /><figcaption><b>Combatientes del Ejército Guerrillero Popular de Liberación </b></figcaption></figure></div>


<p></p>



<p>Tomado de&nbsp;<i><a href="https://dazibaorojo08.blogspot.com/2023/01/indiacondemn-center-and-chhattisgad.html" rel="noreferrer noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Dazibao Rojo</a></i>, 24 de enero 2023</p>



<h4 class="has-text-align-center fittexted_for_content_h4" style="font-size: 27px;">CONTRA LA CORPORATIZACIÓN, MILITARIZACIÓN E HINDUIZACIÓN </h4>



<h5 class="has-text-align-center fittexted_for_content_h5" style="font-size: 22.5px;">(Asuntos Internacionales / Comité Central, Partido Comunista de la India (Maoísta))</h5>



<p>Queridos camaradas, amigos y simpatizantes de la Revolución India, Rojos Saludos.</p>



<p>El 11 de enero 2023 será recordado para siempre en la historia de la 
Revolución India. En la historia revolucionaria india no había habido 
antes disparos de helicópteros sobre nuestro pueblo y revolucionarios en
 el Estado de Chhattisgad, en la selva de Bastar. Aunque nuestros 
guerrilleros del PLGA [Ejército Guerrillero Popular de Liberación] 
resistieron ferozmente e hirieron a 5 comandos Garuda y a un miembro del
 personal técnico de la Fuerza Aérea India. Este es un gran logro en el 
Año Nuevo. El mismo día, en otro lado del estado de Jharkhand, en la 
Selva de Saaranda, las fuerzas CoBRA de las fuerzas paramilitares indias
 iniciaron una gran campaña de operaciones de combate. Allí nuestros 
guerrilleros del PLGA tomaron heroicamente represalias e hirieron 
gravemente a 6 comandos. En esta valiente represalia perdimos a una de 
nuestras queridas camaradas guerrilleras Gangi. Rindamos nuestros 
fecundos homenajes revolucionarios a esa mártir. Nuestro CC, el Partido 
Comunista de la India (Maoísta), hace un llamamiento para condenar estos
 brutales ataques contra los pueblos indígenas de Bastar y Saaranda y 
organizar concentraciones de protesta ante las embajadas indias en el 
llamado Día de la República de la India, es decir, el 26 de enero en 
todo el mundo.</p>



<p>Desde el pasado octubre, las explotadoras clases dominantes indias, 
en particular las fuerzas hindutva, se concentraron fuertemente en el 
movimiento revolucionario indio. Bajo el liderazgo del Ministro del 
Interior indio, el Sr. Amit Sha, se celebró una reunión especial en 
Surajkund, Haryana. En esa reunión, el Primer Ministro indio se dirigió a
 la policía y a los oficiales de inteligencia y les ordenó eliminar a 
los maoístas, tanto a los que tienen armas como a los que tienen 
bolígrafos. Desde hace unos años, las fuerzas Hindutva han introducido 
una nueva expresión en India: naxal urbano. Ahora el Primer Ministro ha 
vuelto a utilizar la misma frase, pero en su propio estilo de discurso 
satírico, Kalamdhari (poseedor de bolígrafos). Por lo tanto, esto indica
 que el Primer Ministro y el Ministro del Interior de la India están muy
 ansiosos por eliminar el movimiento revolucionario indio para cumplir 
sus sueños de Make in India.</p>



<p>El 11 de diciembre, el Ministro del Interior indio celebró una 
reunión crucial de alto nivel con oficiales de inteligencia indios en 
Nueva Delhi y aclaró su plan en esa reunión. Dijo que las fuerzas 
policiales están listas para entrar en la zona de Maad del distrito de 
Narayanpur, Chhattisgad para tomarla bajo su control.</p>



<p>El 7 de enero, el mismo ministro indio del Interior, Amit Sha, se 
dirigió a una reunión pública en Korba, un centro industrial del estado 
de Chhattisgad. En esa reunión declaró que su gobierno creará una India 
libre de maoístas antes de las elecciones generales de 2024. Mientras 
tanto, se llevaron a cabo varias reuniones a nivel local con los 
oficiales de policía para una mejor coordinación de las fuerzas 
estatales y centrales en las próximas operaciones militares.</p>



<p>Por fin, comenzaron sus operaciones de erradicación maoísta en los 
bosques fronterizos de los distritos de Sukma, Bijapur y Dantewada, que 
es la zona colindante con el estado de Telangana. En esta gran operación
 participaron la NSG (National Security Guards), Gray Hounds, CoBRA y 
otras fuerzas armadas de la policía central, junto con los comandos 
Garuda de la Fuerza Aérea India. Pero los guerrilleros del PLGA tomaron 
represalias con el pleno apoyo del pueblo revolucionario local.</p>



<p>Las clases dominantes indias y sus gobiernos centrales y estatales se
 apresuraron a eliminar el Movimiento Revolucionario Indio. Porque, es 
el principal obstáculo en su saqueo y expoliación de los ricos recursos 
del país. Por otro lado, los pueblos indígenas de la India están 
luchando por sus justas reivindicaciones. Luchan por sus derechos 
legales sobre sus bosques. Están en contra de la explotación de los 
bosques. En este contexto, los gobiernos más reaccionarios del centro y 
del estado, que trabajan en beneficio de las grandes corporaciones, 
están lanzando nuevas y mayores formas de ataque contra el pueblo. Por 
ello, nuestro CC [Comité Central] hace un nuevo llamamiento para que 
condenéis estos ataques y alcéis vuestras voces contra las fuerzas 
fascistas Hindutva.</p>



<p>En nuestro país, los intelectuales democráticos y revolucionarios, 
los patriotas, los activistas por los derechos, los estudiantes, los 
jóvenes, los laicistas, los escritores, los artistas, los abogados, los 
defensores de los pueblos indígenas, las fuerzas antiimperialistas 
amantes de la paz y los ecologistas se oponen firmemente a la 
corporatización, la militarización de los bosques y, en particular, a la
 hinduización del país. Por lo tanto, es el momento más favorable para 
desarrollar y consolidar el movimiento de solidaridad revolucionario 
indio en apoyo de la guerra popular en todo el mundo.</p>



Amrut I/C Asuntos Internacionales,&nbsp;<br />CC, PCI (Maoísta)<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h2 itemprop="name">
SP: Metalúrgicos em greve exigem pagamento atrasado com protestos em frente à fábrica </h2>

<div itemprop="articleBody">
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img height="240" src="https://anovademocracia.com.br/images/sidor1.jpg" width="400" /></span></p>
<p><span class="legenda" style="font-weight: 400;">Operários da Sidor realizam protesto após sete dias de greve. Foto: Divulgação</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">No dia 30 de janeiro, os operários da
 Sidor realizaram um protesto em frente às instalações da fábrica. A 
mobilização é parte da greve que iniciou no dia 23/01 para exigir 
pagamento de salários. Os operários da produção estão com os 
vale-salários atrasados desde o dia 20/01, enquanto os trabalhadores da 
administração estão com os salários referentes a dezembro atrasados 
desde o dia 05/01. Por conta da mobilização, os patrões foram também 
obrigados a depositar o décimo terceiro, que estava atrasado.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Não é a primeira vez que os operários
 da Sidor se mobilizam pelo atraso de salários. Em julho e outubro do 
ano passado, os operários da empresa realizaram uma série de greves&nbsp; 
pelos mesmos motivos. Depois das mobilizações, os salários foram 
conquistados. A Sidor tem cerca de 50 trabalhadores e produz autopeças e
 acessórios automobilísticos.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Também em Sorocaba, metalúrgicos da 
empresa Dana realizaram uma paralisação de duas horas no dia 26/01 para 
protestar contra falta de transparência e divergências referente às 
metas e valores da segunda parcela do Programa de Participação nos 
Resultados (PPR) de 2022.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img height="240" src="https://anovademocracia.com.br/images/dana1.jpg" width="400" /></span></p>
<p><span class="legenda" style="font-weight: 400;">Metalúrgicos da Dana paralisam produção por duas horas. Foto: Foguinho/Imprensa SMetal</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Nos últimos anos, diversas 
mobilizações operárias ocorreram em São Paulo contra a perda de direitos
 e por melhores condições de trabalho. Em novembro do ano passado, 
operários conquistaram seus direitos após realizarem ao menos dez greves
 durante uma intensa campanha de aumento salarial real e contra 
demissões em massa. </span></p> </div><p>&nbsp;</p>
